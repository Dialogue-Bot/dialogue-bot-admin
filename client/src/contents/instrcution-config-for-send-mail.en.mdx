Send mail is a common feature in chatbots that allows users to send emails directly from the chatbot interface. By configuring the send mail feature in your chatbot, you can provide users with a convenient way to communicate with you and share information via email.

In this article, we will guide you through the process of configuring the send mail feature in your chatbot. We will cover everything from setting up your email server to connecting it to your chatbot and testing it to ensure that everything is working correctly.

Step 1: Go to your [Google Account](https://myaccount.google.com/).

Step 2: Click on the "Security" tab on the left side of the screen.

Step 3: Under "How you sign in to Google," select 2-Step Verification.

Step 4: At the bottom of the page, select App passwords.

Step 5: Enter a name that helps you remember where you’ll use the app password.

Step 6: Click Generate.

Step 7: Follow the instructions to enter the App password (the 16-character code in the yellow bar) on your device.

Step 8: Copy the generated password and go to [Setting Mail](https://www.dialoguebot.tech/settings/mail)

Step 9: Fill your account email and password that you have just generated. Then click "Save".

Finally, the send mail feature in your chatbot is configured successfully. You can now test it to ensure that everything is working correctly.
