Line is one of the most popular messaging platforms in the world, with over 1.3 billion active users. By configuring the Line channel for your chatbot, you can reach a large audience and provide them with a seamless conversational experience.

In this article, we will guide you through the process of configuring the Line channel for your chatbot. We will cover everything from setting up your Line app to connecting it to your chatbot and testing it to ensure that everything is working correctly.

## Setting up your Line app

Step 1: Go to the [Line Developer platform](https://developers.line.biz/en/) and login your account.

<Img src='/articles/instrcution-config-channel-line/Picture1.png' alt='' />

Step 2: Click on the "Create a new provider" button and fill in the required information to create a new provider.

<Img
  src='/articles/instrcution-config-channel-line/Picture2.png'
  alt='Create App'
/>

Step 3: After creating a new provider, click on the "Create a Messaging API channel" button to create a new channel.

<Img
  src='/articles/instrcution-config-channel-line/Picture3.png'
  alt='Select type of your app'
/>

Step 4: Fill in the required information for your app. And then your app will be created.

Step 5: On tab click tab "Messaging API" and scroll down to "Channel access token" section to copy the "Channel access token".

<Img
  src='/articles/instrcution-config-channel-line/Picture4.png'
  alt='Channel access token'
/>

<Img
  src='/articles/instrcution-config-channel-line/Picture5.png'
  alt='Channel access token'
/>

Step 6: Go to the [Page channel](https://www.dialoguebot.tech/channels) to fill in the required information and click "Add new channel".

<Img
  src='/articles/instrcution-config-channel-line/Picture6.png'
  alt='Create App'
/>

Finally, your Line channel is configured successfully. You can now test your chatbot on the Line platform to ensure that everything is working correctly.
